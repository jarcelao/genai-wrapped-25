# GenAI Industry Wrapped 2025 (YTD) - Research Brief

## Mission
Gather comprehensive data on the GenAI industry from January 1, 2025 to December 11, 2025, with a specific focus on Large Language Models (LLMs) in business enterprise and software engineering contexts. This research will power an interactive "Wrapped" experience for an engineering team audience.

## Time Scope
- **Primary period:** January 1, 2025 - December 11, 2025
- **Context:** Include developments from late 2024 only if critical to understanding 2025 trends

## Geographic Scope
- **Primary:** Global coverage
- **Secondary:** Dedicated subsection on APAC region developments (China, Japan, Southeast Asia, Australia/NZ, India)

## Focus Areas
Research should concentrate on LLMs specifically used for:
1. **Business enterprise applications** - productivity, knowledge management, customer service, internal tools
2. **Software engineering use cases** - code generation, debugging, testing, documentation, DevOps

---

## Data Categories to Research

### 1. Model Releases & Technical Evolution
- New LLM releases from major providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Mistral, Cohere, etc.)
- Model capability breakthroughs and benchmark improvements (coding benchmarks, MMLU, HumanEval, etc.)
- Context window expansions and efficiency improvements
- Multimodal capabilities added to LLMs
- Open source vs. proprietary model trends
- Notable model deprecations or sunsets
- Pricing changes and cost-per-token evolution
- Inference speed and latency improvements

### 2. Company Milestones & Market Dynamics
- Major funding rounds in the GenAI space (Series A through late stage)
- Acquisitions and mergers involving GenAI companies
- IPOs or significant liquidity events
- Company shutdowns, pivots, or major restructurings
- New market entrants and notable startups launched
- Partnerships between tech giants and AI companies
- Revenue milestones and valuation changes for key players
- Strategic shifts in business models

### 3. Enterprise Adoption & Use Cases
- Notable enterprise deployments or case studies
- Industry-specific adoption patterns (finance, healthcare, legal, manufacturing, retail, etc.)
- ROI metrics and productivity gains reported by enterprises
- Common implementation challenges and solutions
- Shift in enterprise buying patterns (DIY vs. vendor solutions)
- Integration trends with existing enterprise software (Microsoft 365, Salesforce, Slack, ServiceNow, etc.)
- Security and compliance developments for enterprise AI
- Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) insights

### 4. Software Engineering Revolution
- Developer tool launches and updates (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Replit, Codeium, etc.)
- IDE integrations and new development workflows
- Adoption statistics for AI coding assistants (active users, market share)
- Impact on developer productivity (studies, surveys, company reports)
- Evolution of AI-assisted testing, debugging, and code review
- Infrastructure and platform engineering developments
- DevOps and CI/CD automation advances
- Agentic coding tools and autonomous development systems

### 5. Research Breakthroughs
- Significant academic papers that moved the industry forward
- Novel architectures or training techniques
- Improvements in reasoning, planning, and agentic capabilities
- Advances in model efficiency and inference optimization
- Breakthroughs in safety, alignment, or interpretability
- Research from major labs (DeepMind, OpenAI Research, Anthropic, Meta FAIR, Microsoft Research, etc.)
- Innovations in fine-tuning, RAG, and prompt engineering
- Memory and context management improvements

### 6. Regulatory & Policy Landscape
- AI regulation developments globally (EU AI Act implementation, US executive orders, UK approaches, etc.)
- Copyright and intellectual property rulings or legislation
- Data privacy regulations affecting AI systems (GDPR, state-level US laws)
- Export controls and geopolitical tensions around AI (US-China dynamics)
- Industry self-regulation initiatives
- Government AI adoption and procurement policies
- Liability and accountability frameworks emerging

### 7. Controversies & Challenges
- High-profile incidents (hallucinations causing problems, security breaches, misuse cases)
- Ethical debates and public discourse moments
- Leadership controversies or boardroom drama
- Model bias or safety incidents
- Environmental concerns and compute sustainability debates
- Workforce displacement concerns and labor union responses
- Academic integrity and education system challenges
- Misinformation and deepfake concerns

### 8. Key People & Leadership
- Executive movements (CEOs, CTOs, Chief Scientists hired/departed)
- Influential researchers changing organizations
- Prominent voices emerging in the space (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, conference speakers)
- Leadership shake-ups at major AI companies
- Notable conference speakers and thought leadership
- Influential blog posts, papers, or public statements
- Industry "celebrity" moments

### 9. Market Metrics & Industry Health
- Total market size estimates and growth rates
- Usage statistics (API calls, active users, tokens processed) where publicly available
- Compute and infrastructure spending trends (GPU demand, cloud costs)
- Investment trends (VC activity levels, investor sentiment, deal sizes)
- Talent market dynamics (hiring trends, salary data, skill demand)
- Customer acquisition costs and unit economics insights where disclosed
- Market consolidation vs. fragmentation trends
- Stock price movements for publicly traded AI companies

### 10. APAC Region Deep Dive
Apply all above categories with specific focus on:
- **China:** Domestic LLM ecosystem (Baidu, Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, etc.), regulatory environment
- **Southeast Asia:** Market adoption patterns, local language model development, startup ecosystem, particularly in the Philippines, Thailand, and Singapore
- **Regional differences:** Regulatory approaches, cultural adoption patterns, infrastructure challenges

## Output Requirements

### Format
Structure the research to support an interactive, story-driven presentation with:
- **Clear narrative arcs** - what changed, why it matters, what comes next
- **Quantitative data points** suitable for data visualization (charts, graphs, timelines)
- **Qualitative insights** that capture the "feel" and zeitgeist of 2025
- **Surprising facts** or counterintuitive findings that challenge assumptions
- **Month-by-month timeline** of major events for temporal visualization
- **Comparative data** - year-over-year comparisons, before/after metrics, competitive benchmarks
- **Trend analysis** - what's accelerating, decelerating, or plateauing

### Tone
- **Technical but accessible** for an engineering audience
- Assume readers understand how LLMs work but may not follow industry news closely
- Balance technical depth with storytelling
- Highlight engineering-relevant implications

- **Primary sources:** Company announcements, blog posts, research papers, SEC filings
- **Tech journalism:** The Verge, TechCrunch, The Information, Bloomberg, Reuters
- **Industry analysts:** Gartner, Forrester, CB Insights, IDC
- **Academic publications:** arXiv, conference proceedings (NeurIPS, ICML, ACL)
- **Company earnings calls** and investor presentations
- **Developer surveys** (Stack Overflow, GitHub, JetBrains)

## Key Questions to Answer

1. **What were the defining moments that shaped GenAI in 2025?**
   - Which 3-5 events had the most lasting impact?

2. **How did enterprise AI adoption accelerate or evolve?**
   - Did adoption meet, exceed, or fall short of expectations?
   - What barriers were overcome? What new barriers emerged?

3. **What changed in how developers work with code assistants?**
   - How has the developer workflow fundamentally shifted?
   - What percentage of code is now AI-assisted?

4. **Which companies won big, and which struggled?**
   - Who gained market share? Who lost it?
   - Were there unexpected winners or losers?

5. **What technical breakthroughs matter most for the next 12 months?**
   - Which innovations will compound into major advantages?
   - What became possible in 2025 that wasn't before?

6. **How did the global vs. APAC landscape differ?**
   - Are APAC markets ahead, behind, or diverging from the West?
   - What unique innovations came from APAC?

7. **What predictions from early 2025 came true? Which were completely wrong?**
   - Review analyst predictions from Q4 2024/Q1 2025
   - Score the accuracy of industry forecasts

8. **What surprised industry insiders the most?**
   - Find quotes from experts on unexpected developments
   - Identify consensus-breaking trends

9. **How did costs evolve?**
   - Did AI get more expensive or cheaper for enterprises?
   - What's the ROI reality vs. the hype?

10. **What's the state of the "AI engineering" job market?**
    - New roles created? Skills in demand?
    - Salary trends and hiring velocity?
